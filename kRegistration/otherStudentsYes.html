Click Here to access the RSU 71 Parent Portal to register your student for Kindergarten. We already have you in our system so the application will pull all of your existing information, saving you time.
Once logged in
  • Click More(or the 3 bars at the top left if you do not see the More link)
  • Annual Student Update
  • Use the EXISTING Student Option - for 2023-2024 School Year.
  • When you get to the "Student Section" of the application click Add New Student to Register your student for Kindergarten. Also, complete the info for any other students in your household.
